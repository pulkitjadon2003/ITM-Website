Mentor Mentee Scheme

Objective

Mentor-Mentee System of the institute has been implemented with an objective to bridge the gap between the faculty members and students and provide guidance on the academic and professional development matters.

GUIDELINES FOR IMPLEMENTATION OF MENTOR-MENTEE SCHEME

  • UG I year students/mentee will be assigned student mentors from II and III year undergraduate program and a faculty mentor.
  • There will be a group of 05 to 13 mentees under each faculty mentor whom they serve as mentors.
  • After the allotment of mentees, every faculty mentor will collect the information as per Annexure-I through Google form.The template of the same is shared at following link:
  • Each faculty mentor will conduct meeting twice in a month to understand and counsel the mentees on the existing academic policies, extracurricular activities or personal matters.
  • The report of activity will be submitted by faculty mentor as per Annexure-II at following link : https://forms.gle/a2sLB3wP3S5AdxEm9

ROLE OF A FACULTY MENTOR

  • To take the lead in supporting a mentee for his/her welfare through one-to-one relationship.
  • To build a relationship of trust by caring and planning.
  • To serve as a positive role model.
  • To motivate mentees for interdisciplinary research/innovation within a group.

DESIRABLE QUALITIES OF A MENTOR

  • Good listener
  • Encouraging and supportive
  • Patient and flexible

RESPONSIBILITIES OF A FACULTY MENTOR

  • Meet the mentee students twice in a month either online or offline.
  • Make the mentees aware of existing system of the institute related to academic, professional development and guide on personal matters as well.
  • Discover talents and interests of mentees and encourage them to exhibit them by participating in various activities.
  • Motivate the students to follow ethics, good practices and universal human values.
  • Guide mentees regarding the importance of training programs, internships, industry visits, studentsâ€™ committees at the institute, club activities, seminar, workshops, conferences, examination norms, general structure of the scheme, MOOC courses etc.
  • Keep the record of academic and other professional activities of mentees.
  • Assess the performance of Student Mentors on the basis of their sincerity and contribution in activities assigned to them.
  • Contact parents/guardians if situation demands e.g. irregularities, negative behavioral changes and interpersonal relations, detrimental activities etc.

ROLE OF STUDENT MENTOR

  • To help the mentees in their holistic development and growth.
  • To act as a link between faculty mentor and mentees.
  • To attend fortnightly meeting regularly and encourage mentees for the same.
  • To contribute to the development of the Mentor-Mentee Scheme by suggesting new ideas.

RESPONSIBILITIES OF A MENTEE

  • Respect the mentor
  • Regularly attend the meetings with the mentor and strictly follow the instructions given by mentors.
  • Keep the mentors updated regarding your academic and other professional developments.

RESPONSIBILITIES OF THE INSTITUTE COORDINATOR OF MENTOR- MENTEE SYSTEM

  • Allocate mentors to all I year UG students.
  • Support the mentors for effective implementation of mentor-mentee system at the institute level.
  • Submit the annual report to competent authority.
